What We Do

The official employers' organisation of Indonesia, representing businesses in industrial relations, manpower affairs, and economic policy — everything you need to know.

Industrial Relations

Facilitating harmonious relations between employers and employees to foster productive workplaces across Indonesia.

Manpower Affairs

Advocating fair labour standards, workforce development, and social protection policies for a resilient economy.

Economic Policy

Engaging government and stakeholders to shape policies that stimulate sustainable business growth nationwide.

Global Partnerships

Building international business networks and advocating for Indonesia in global trade forums, including EU-CEPA.
